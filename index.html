Connected Devices

The app works as a system hub. Real user interaction mainly happens through voice guidance, watch vibration, earphones, glasses, and simple controls.

CameraShows the live rear camera view and supports continuous estimated obstacle guidance on the navigation screen.
EarphonesPlays short English guidance such as “1.4 metres. Turn left.”
SmartwatchProvides graded vibration: light above 2m, medium from 1–2m, strong below 1m.
AppRuns in the background as the system center for device coordination, AI support, and profile settings.
